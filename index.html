About me

Fang Qin joined the School of Control Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University in 2021 to pursue her Ph.D. under the supervision of Researcher Lu Haojian (Overseas High-Level Talent) and co-supervision of Professor Gong Zhefeng. From 2018 to 2021, she completed her master's degree at the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Zhejiang University, where her research focused on origami-inspired soft robotics. Her peer-reviewed work has been published in leading international journals such as IEEE Transactions on Mechatronics, Research, Advanced Intelligent Systems, Sensors and Actuators A: Physical, among others. She has received several honors, including the Zhejiang University Interdisciplinary Research Achievement Award and the Wiley China Excellent Author Prize.
