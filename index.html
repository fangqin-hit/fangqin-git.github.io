About me

Fang Qin joined the School of Control Science and Engineering of Zhejiang University in 2021 to pursue her PhD under the supervision of Researcher Lu Haojian (Overseas Youqing), and her co-supervisor is Professor Gong Zhefeng. Previously, she studied for a master's degree at the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics of Zhejiang University from 2018 to 2021, where she studied origami-inspired soft robots. Her peer-reviewed papers have been published in international journals including IEEE Transactions on Mechatronics, Research, Advanced Intelligent Systems, Sensors and Actuators A: Physical, and et al.
